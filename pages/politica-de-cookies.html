Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 July 2026

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser when you visit a website. They allow the site to recognise your device, remember preferences and collect statistical data about how the page is used.

Types of cookies we use

We organise World MMA's cookies into four categories:

CategoryPurposeCan it be switched off?
Strictly necessary Enable essential site functionality, such as navigation and session preferences. No
Performance and analytics Collect aggregated data on site usage (e.g. Google Analytics 4, Microsoft Clarity) to help us understand browsing behaviour. Yes
Advertising and measurement Used by media partners (e.g. Meta Pixel, Snap Pixel, Pinterest Tag) to measure the effectiveness of campaigns. Yes
Functionality Remember preferences such as the news category visited or the state of interface components. Yes

Third-party tools set up on this portal

The site's technical structure is set up to integrate with the following measurement tools, which may set their own cookies when enabled:

  • Google Analytics 4
  • Google Tag Manager
  • Meta Pixel
  • Pinterest Tag
  • Snap Pixel
  • Microsoft Clarity

Each of these tools has its own privacy policy, which we recommend consulting directly on the respective provider's website.

How to manage cookies

You can manage or disable cookies directly in your browser settings. Disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality. Here's how to manage cookies in the most popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings → Privacy and security → Cookies;
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options → Privacy & Security;
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy;
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings → Cookies and site permissions.

Changes to this policy

This Cookie Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The date at the top of this page indicates the most recent version.
