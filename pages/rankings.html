Content checked 14 July 2026 — rankings change weekly

New system: Meta UFC Rankings

On 20 June 2026, the UFC began replacing the traditional media voting panel with the Meta UFC Rankings, a mathematical Elo-based model designed to reduce subjectivity by factoring in opponent quality, recent activity, and the decay of old wins or inactive fighters. The media panel continues to operate only during the transition period between the two systems.
