Independent content

Our mission

We believe MMA fans deserve quality coverage: fast, well-researched and free of sensationalism. World MMA was created as an editorial space dedicated entirely to the sport — bringing together news, technical analysis, interviews and ranking data organised clearly.

Editorial independence

We are not affiliated with, sponsored by, or an official representative of any fighting organisation, including the UFC®, PFL®, ONE Championship® and Bellator®. All published content is produced independently by our editorial team, based on our own research and public sources.

How we work

Our team follows the national and international MMA scene to bring context, not just headlines. We prioritise accuracy, transparency about sources, and public corrections whenever necessary.

Our principles

What guides our work

Transparency

We make it clear when content is opinion, analysis or factual news — never blurring the lines between formats.

Responsible speed

We aim to be fast without sacrificing careful fact-checking before publication.

Independence

No commercial or institutional ties to fighting organisations — our only obligation is to the reader.
